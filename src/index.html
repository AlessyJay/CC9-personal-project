Why GREENT?

GREENT Travel is a forward-thinking travel company headquartered in London born out of a passion to do things differently.

In Swahili, a Bantu language spoken by millions in Eastern, Central and Southern Africa, the name Niara means "with utmost purpose"

and our team are united by a passion for exploration and a belief that the right kind of travel can make the world a better place.

Our Travel Researchers are here to create incredible trips with a positive impact for you.

Unforgettable experiences

Creating your trip is entirely collaborative and our Travel Researchers are here for every step.

Positive impact

Curated sustainable experiences and accommodation that maximize the benefits to local people and place.

Africa

The greatest wildlife spectacle on earth

Africa is the world’s premier wildlife watching destination.

From coming face to face with a troop of gorillas in the jungle and feeling a million wildebeest hooves thunder across the Serengeti on their migration,

to following big cats on the prowl through the bush and breathtaking encounters elephants on foot, there is nowhere like it.

From: £4,196

Europe

Luxury nature travel and safaris

Europe may not be the first continent that springs to mind when looking for a luxury nature-based holiday,

yet there are areas of true wilderness to be found close to its historic cities, and regions where nature is being encouraged to flourish once more.

From: £3,916

Asia

Ancient cultures and vital wilderness

Whether you're hiking on the Roof of World in the Himalayas, tracking tigers in the jungle,

island hopping by sailing yacht or diving with whale sharks, Asia’s wild places are as epic as they are varied.

From: £3,182

Australia

Unique wildlife and unlimited adventure

New Zealand, Australia and a sprinkling of Polynesian islands in the Pacific between them are incredible settings for an adventure.

Outback deserts, rainbow coral playgrounds, alpine mountains, and green forests home to some of the world’s most unusual wildlife –there are countless stretches of beautiful wilderness to explore.

From: £10,472

Latin America

Diverse landscapes, cultures and wildlife

From great rainforests and wetlands bursting with life to snowcapped Patagonian mountains and red deserts, all skirted by the rich waters of the Atlantic and Pacific Oceans, Latin America is a stunningly diverse continent.

Its cultures too are colourful and varied with a beautiful blend of indigenous, European and African influences to experience. Climb ancient wonders and wander colonial-era plazas before indulging in buzzing creative and culinary scenes.

From: £3,515
